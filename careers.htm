About Lakeside

Lakeside Gas Company Inc. is a licensed residential gas and mechanical services company serving Peterborough and surrounding areas. We specialize in gas line installations, generator hook-ups, appliance installations, inspections, and mechanical system upgrades. As we expand our residential HVAC division, we are seeking a licensed 313D to join our growing team.

Position Overview

Install, service, maintain, and troubleshoot residential HVAC equipment including air conditioners, heat pumps, and related components. Work alongside other skilled trades to deliver clean, code-compliant installations and professional customer experiences.

Required Qualifications

  • Valid 313D Residential Air Conditioning Mechanic License (Ontario)
  • Valid ODP Certification
  • Valid G Driver’s License (clean abstract preferred)
  • 2+ years field experience (preferred)
  • Strong understanding of diagnostics, brazing, line sets, and commissioning
  • Ability to read mechanical drawings and manufacturer manuals
  • Working knowledge of TSSA/ESA coordination when required

Preferred (Assets)

  • 313A (asset)
  • Gas Technician License (asset)
  • Working at Heights
  • WHMIS
  • First Aid / CPR

Core Responsibilities

  • Residential A/C and heat pump installations
  • System start-ups, performance testing, and commissioning
  • Refrigerant recovery, charging, and leak diagnostics
  • Service and troubleshooting calls
  • Customer education and walkthrough at job completion
  • Maintain a clean, organized, safe job site
  • Accurate documentation of work performed

Location: Peterborough & Surrounding Areas • Type: Full-Time / Contract

About the Role

Lakeside Gas Company Inc. is expanding into solid-fuel appliance installs and inspections. We’re seeking a WETT Certified Technician to complete wood-burning appliance installations, venting systems, and WETT inspections with high documentation standards and safety-first execution.

Required Qualifications

  • Active WETT Certification in good standing (Installer and/or Inspector designation required)
  • Valid driver’s license
  • 2+ years experience with wood stoves, inserts, chimneys, liners, and venting systems
  • Strong understanding of clearances, hearth protection, and solid-fuel venting principles
  • Ability to conduct Level 1 & Level 2 inspections and produce detailed inspection reports
  • Ability to identify code deficiencies, safety risks, and provide clear corrective recommendations

Preferred (Assets)

  • Gas Technician License (asset)
  • Working at Heights / Fall Protection
  • WHMIS
  • First Aid / CPR

Core Responsibilities

  • Install wood-burning appliances and associated venting systems
  • Chimney liner installations and venting upgrades
  • Clearance verification and corrections (as required)
  • Level 1 and Level 2 WETT inspections
  • Issue formal inspection documentation and photo records
  • Maintain clean job sites and professional customer communication
  • Coordinate with other trades when required for complete scope delivery

Standards & Compliance

All work must follow manufacturer installation instructions and applicable Ontario requirements, and be executed to WETT inspection and reporting standards with clear documentation for homeowners, insurers, and real estate transactions (when applicable).
